CARSON HYDE

Carson was born in Bloomington, IN before being carted across the country to sleepy, semi-rural Lakeland, FL. Aftering fleeing the south at 22, Carson lived in Los Angeles and San Francisco before finally settling in Brooklyn.

Carson has worked as a butcher/fish monger, e-commerce content writer, sandwich artist (very briefly due to a tendency to make ugly sandwiches), health insurance service rep, HR coordinator, and at numerous smoothie shops.

Carson studied philosophy and gender studies at the University of South Florida before dropping out and heading west. Carson is currently enrolled in a web development bootcamp at General Assembly NYC and in a BS program at Western Governor's University in HR Management.
